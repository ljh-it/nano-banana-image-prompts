# YouTube Thumbnails

## T1 — Bold Face + Clean Background + Text Area
Prompt:
High-contrast YouTube thumbnail, close-up expressive face, clean vibrant background, strong rim light, sharp focus, leave empty space on the right for bold text, punchy colors, crisp edges.

## T2 — “Before/After” Split Composition
Prompt:
Split-screen thumbnail composition: left side "before" dull lighting, right side "after" vibrant cinematic lighting, clear separation line, big visual contrast, space for labels, sharp details.

## T3 — Product Reaction Thumbnail
Prompt:
YouTube thumbnail of a person reacting to a product, exaggerated surprised expression, product large in foreground, bright studio lighting, high saturation, clean background, space for headline text.

## T4 — Tech Review Studio Look
Prompt:
Tech review thumbnail, modern studio background with soft gradients, key light + rim light, 35mm lens, subject centered, product on left, leave top area for title text, crisp and clean.

## T5 — Meme-ish Bold Outline Style (Still Realistic)
Prompt:
Realistic photo base with subtle bold edge separation, high contrast, bright colors, subject pop-out effect, simple background, big negative space for text.

## T6 — “Top 10” List Thumbnail
Prompt:
Thumbnail collage layout: 3 items arranged diagonally, clean separators, high clarity, bright lighting, leave top-left for "TOP 10" text, crisp product render look.

## T7 — Dramatic Shadow Hook
Prompt:
Cinematic thumbnail, dramatic side lighting casting strong shadow, subject half-lit, mysterious background, large empty area for text overlay, sharp focus.

## T8 — Gaming Style Neon
Prompt:
Neon gaming thumbnail, vibrant magenta/cyan lighting, subject with intense expression, glow accents, clean composition, big text area, sharp details.

## T9 — Tutorial Screenshot Style
Prompt:
Clean tutorial thumbnail, minimal background, clear subject + simple icon elements, high readability, flat lighting, leave center space for step numbers.

## T10 — “Comparison Battle” Layout
Prompt:
Two subjects facing each other, center lightning bolt separator, strong contrast, clean background, room for labels above each subject, bold composition.
