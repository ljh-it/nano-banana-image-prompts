# Posters & Infographics

## I1 — Clean Infographic Layout (4 Blocks)
Prompt:
Create a clean infographic poster with a 4-block grid layout, clear section titles, simple icons, plenty of whitespace, consistent typography hierarchy, modern flat design, high readability.

## I2 — Product Feature Poster (3 Key Points)
Prompt:
A modern product feature poster, big hero product in the center, three callout labels with lines pointing to features, clean background, space reserved for headline at top, crisp layout.

## I3 — Roadmap Timeline Poster
Prompt:
A visual timeline infographic with 5 milestones, left-to-right flow, consistent icon style, clean spacing, readable labels, modern tech aesthetic.

## I4 — “How it Works” Diagram
Prompt:
A “How it works” diagram poster, 3-step flow with arrows, minimal iconography, clear step numbers, modern UI style, high readability.

## I5 — Price Plan Comparison Card
Prompt:
A pricing comparison visual: 3 cards side by side, clear plan names, feature list bullets, highlight middle plan, clean typography, modern SaaS style.

## I6 — Education Flashcard Sheet
Prompt:
A printable flashcard sheet layout, 6 cards per page, clear titles, simple illustrations, high contrast for readability, minimal design.

## I7 — Scientific Style Figure
Prompt:
A scientific figure layout with labeled parts, clean lines, subtle grid background, consistent annotations, professional research paper style.

## I8 — Social Media Carousel Slide
Prompt:
A social media carousel slide: bold headline, supporting bullet points, simple illustration, consistent margins, modern design system look.
